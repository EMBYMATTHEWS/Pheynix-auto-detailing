# EMBY MATTHEWS - Global Music Distribution Strategy

## 🚀 PHASE 1: Distribution Setup (Weeks 1-2)

### Select Distribution Service
Choose ONE distributor to upload your music:

**Recommended Options:**

| Service | Cost | Features |
|---------|------|----------|
| **DistroKid** | $23/year unlimited uploads | Keep 100% royalties, fastest upload |
| **TuneCore** | $100/year per single | Good analytics, multiple stores |
| **CD Baby** | $9.95 per single, 9% fee | One-time upload, publishing admin |
| **Landr** | $10/month | Mastering included, Spotify playlisting |
| **Symphonic** | Free 80% royalties | Good for indie artists |

**Quick Decision:** Start with **DistroKid** for unlimited uploads and 100% royalties

### Upload Process
1. Create account on distributor
2. Upload tracks (WAV/MP3, 44.1kHz/24-bit)
3. Add metadata (title, artist, genre, lyrics)
4. Choose artwork (3000x3000px, JPEG/PNG)
5. Set release date (4-6 weeks ahead minimum)
6. Pay upload fee

### Stores to Target
- Spotify
- Apple Music
- YouTube Music
- Amazon Music
- Deezer
- Tidal
- Pandora

**Timeline:** 4-6 weeks before release

## 📱 PHASE 2: Artist Profiles (Weeks 1-4)

### Spotify Artist Profile
**URL:** open.spotify.com/artist/[Your-ID]

**Steps:**
1. Claim profile through Spotify for Artists
2. Upload artist bio (200-500 words)
3. Add high-quality artist photos (1500x1500px recommended)
3. Create artist playlist with your tracks
4. Pitch upcoming release to Spotify's curation team

**Link to claim:** https://artists.spotify.com/

### Apple Music for Artists
**URL:** music.apple.com/artist/

**Steps:**
1. Claim profile through distributor or directly
2. Upload artist bio and photos
3. Connect social media accounts
4. Submit artist to Apple Music playlist curators

### YouTube Channel
**Steps:**
1. Create channel "EMBY MATTHEWS"
2. Upload music videos (1080p minimum)
3. Upload lyric videos
4. Create channel trailer

## 📊 PHASE 4: Growth Strategy (Ongoing)

### Playlist Pitching Strategy

**Tier 1 - Major Editorial Playlists:**
- Submit through Spotify for Artists dashboard
- Pitch 3 weeks before release
- Focus on: "New Music Friday", "RapCaviar", "Today's Top Hits"
- Target: 10-100k+ followers

**Tier 2 - Independent Playlists:**
- Submit to SubmitHub (free and paid options)
- Use PlaylistPush for paid placements
- Pitch to 50+ playlists per release
- Target: 1000-50k followers each

**Tier 3 - User Playlists:**
- Search for playlists with your song
- DM playlist creators
- Join "playlist networking" Facebook groups
- Offer free promotional exchanges

**Weekly Target:** 20+ playlist submissions

### Content Calendar (Weekly)

| Day | Content Type | Platform |
|-----|--------------|----------|
| Monday | Behind-the-scenes | Instagram Stories |
| Wednesday | Song snippet | TikTok/Reels |
| Friday | Release update | All platforms |
| Sunday | Q&A session | Twitter Spaces |

### Monthly Content:
- 1 single or 1 album track
- 4-6 TikTok/Reels videos
- 2 music videos or lyric videos
