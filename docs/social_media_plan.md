# EMBY MATTHEWS - Social Media Launch Plan

## 🚀 Launch Strategy: 30 Days to Global Reach

### Week 1: Foundation
**Days 1-3: Profile Setup**
- Create TikTok @emby.matthews
- Create Instagram @emby.matthews
- Create YouTube @emby.matthews
- Create Twitter @emby.matthews
- Claim Spotify for Artists

**Days 4-7: First Content**
- Post 1 teaser clip on TikTok

### Week 2: Momentum
**Daily TikTok/Reels**
- 1 short video per day (15-30 seconds)
- Show: Studio sessions, songwriting, production
- Use trending audio when possible
- Post between 6-9 PM local time

**Instagram Stories**
- Daily updates on music process
- Polls and Q&A stickers
- Share behind-the-scenes photos
- Link in bio to streaming platforms

### Week 3: Engagement
**Collaboration Push**
- Reach out to 10 artists for collab
- Comment on 20 similar artist posts daily
- Join music Facebook groups
- Engage with fans who interact

**Playlist Pitching**
- Submit to SubmitHub free tier
- Pitch to indie playlists on Spotify
- Share your Spotify artist profile
- Ask fans to add to their playlists

### Week 4: Growth
**First Release Strategy**
- Release your first single
- Share pre-save link everywhere
- Run TikTok challenge with your song
- Email all contacts about release

**Social Media Ads** ($100 budget)
- Instagram ads: $50

- Target fans of similar artists

## 🎯 Viral Content Formula

**The 3-Second Hook:**
Every video needs to grab attention in first 3 seconds

**Hook Examples:**
- "This is why my song hit 100k streams"
- "Watch me produce this in 30 seconds"
- "The truth about being a musician"
- "What nobody tells new artists"

**The Content Structure:**
1. Hook (3 seconds) - grab attention
2. Value (15-30 seconds) - deliver insight
3. CTA (5 seconds) - "Follow for more"

## 💡 Growth Hacks

### Hack #1: The 1000 True Fans
Find 1000 fans who buy everything:
- Create exclusive content for superfans
- Offer 1-on-1 calls for top 100 fans
- Send physical merch to first 100 fans

### Hack #2: Collab Collab Collab
Collaborate with artists of similar size:
- Feature each other on tracks
- Cross-promote on social media
- Guest on each other's playlists
- Live stream together

### Hack #3: Trending Audio
Use trending sounds early:
- Monitor TikTok trending page daily
- Put your spin on trending audio
- Use trending audio within first 48 hours

### Hack #4: Comment Strategy
Engage before and after posting:
- Comment on 50 similar artist posts before posting
- Reply to ALL comments on your posts within 1 hour
- Engage with fans who comment

### Hack #5: The "Just One Song" Theory
Every song should have:
- 10-20 TikTok/Reels clips
- 3-4 lyric videos
- 1 official music video
- 1 behind-the-scenes video
- Multiple social media posts
