# Pheynix Auto Detailing - Lead Generation Guide

## Target Customer Segments

### 🏢 HIGH VALUE: Car Dealerships
- **Who**: Service departments, CPO (Certified Pre-Owned) programs
- **Pitch**: "Get your trade-in and demo vehicles looking showroom-ready"
- **Value**: $200-$500 per vehicle, recurring contracts
- **How to find**:
  - Google "car dealerships near [your area]"
  - Target franchises: Honda, Toyota, Ford, BMW, Mercedes
  - Ask for "Fleet Manager" or "Service Director"

### 🚗 HIGH VALUE: Rental Car Companies
- **Who**: Enterprise, Hertz, Avis, Budget locations
- **Pitch**: "Quick turn-around detailing between rentals"
- **Value**: $200-$300 per vehicle, daily volume
- **How to find**:
  - Walk into rental locations during off-peak hours
  - Ask for "Fleet Services Manager"
  - Highlight speed and consistency

### 💎 PREMIUM: Luxury Car Owners
- **Who**: Tesla, BMW, Mercedes, Audi owners
- **Pitch**: "Premium detailing for your luxury investment"
- **Value**: $200-$400 per service
- **How to find**:
  - Target Tesla Service Centers
  - High-end residential areas
  - Country club parking lots
  - Instagram ads with before/after photos

### 📱 MEDIUM: Ride-Share Drivers
- **Who**: Uber/Lyft drivers with multiple vehicles
- **Pitch**: "Monthly maintenance packages for your fleet"
- **Value**: $200 per car, recurring revenue
- **How to find**:
  - Uber/Lyft driver meetups
  - Partner with local coffee shops near rideshare hubs
  - Offer "driver loyalty discounts"

### 🏗️ BULK: Fleet Owners
- **Who**: Local delivery services, landscaping, maintenance companies
- **Pitch**: "Bulk contracts with volume discounts"
- **Value**: $200+ per vehicle, monthly contracts
- **How to find**:
  - Search "local delivery companies"
  - Target landscaping and maintenance fleets
  - Offer 10% discount for 10+ vehicles/month

## Quick-Action Lead Sources

### 1. Google Business Profile
- Search: "car detailing" + your city
- Look for businesses with low ratings = opportunity to beat them
- Call and ask for their current detailing supplier

### 2. Yelp & Review Sites
- Search: "car wash" + your city
- Read 1-2 star reviews for pain points
- Reach out saying: "I noticed customers complain about X - we solve that"

### 3. Facebook Marketplace & Groups
- Post: "Professional car detailing - $200 special"
- Join local "Cars & Trucks" groups
- Offer first-time customer discount

### 4. Nextdoor App
- Post in your neighborhood
- "New detailing business serving [your area]"
- Offer neighborhood discount for referrals

### 5. Instagram & TikTok
- Post before/after transformations
- Use hashtags: #[YourCity]Cars, #[YourCity]Detailing
- Tag luxury car owners in your area

## Cold Outreach Script (Phone)

```
"Hi, this is [Name] from Pheynix Auto Detailing. 

We specialize in premium vehicle refresh services. 

I'm calling to offer our new customers a special $200 total vehicle refresh package that includes exterior wash AND full interior deep vacuum.

Are you currently using a car detailing service for your vehicles?

[If yes]
What are you paying now, and how satisfied are you with the results?

[If no]
Most of our customers come from referrals. Would you like to see a quick before/after of what a $200 refresh can do?"
```

## Conversion Tips

1. **Speed**: Respond to inquiries within 1 hour
2. **Photos**: Always send before/after photos
3. **Testimonials**: Collect reviews from first 3 customers
4. **Referral Program**: Offer $20 off for every referral
5. **Repeat**: Set up monthly maintenance for satisfied customers
